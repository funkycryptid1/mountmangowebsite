The Mystery of Mount Mango - aka "Mount Mango" (the comic)

Mount Mango can refer to the comic series, or the setting where the story "The Mystery of Mount Mango" takes place.

If you're looking to read about the setting of the comic, click here.



The Mystery of Mount Mango is an upcoming 2026 webcomic written and drawn by Michael Wolf. It's shorthand is Mount Mango.

It began production around late 2019 as an RPG-Maker game, but due to the author's head-in-the-clouds approach of making

a multi-choice expansive roleplaying experience, which wouldn't have worked well for a first-time indie developer, he

opted to make a webcomic instead.


For about how many years it's been since 2019, Michael has drafted and planned a story that centers on four main protagonists:

Nio Eukakio, Melody Rivers, Calvin Cadence, and Dimitri Delgado. They meet each other early into the story and agree to go on

the adventure of a lifetime together, each aiming to achieve their own goals throughout the fantasy world.




The comic is currently in production, with an expected seven chapters to drop this year, the first part of the first arc.

There are three planned arcs for the entirety of the story.
