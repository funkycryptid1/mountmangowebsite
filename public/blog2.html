    Blog 2: future plans for the website and comic rollout

    June 14th, 2026 - About 5:44 PM

    Right now it's raining and I like the weather! When I'm inside, that is. I faintly hear the World Cup playing in the living room. I like how excited my mom gets when her preferred team gets a goal. Anyway, comic talk... Yeah, yeah...

    In a very short amount of time, I made a lot of progress on this thing, way more than I could have ever imagined. I got the site up with pages that work when you click the buttons (what a feat, yes!) but that's something that was super unimaginable for me like last month. But it just goes to show if you try, you'll never know what you could do. In this blog entry I wanna talk about my ideas for the site (which is mostly where I'd like it to be, save for the comic not being here yet) and the comic's rollout.

    For the homepage, that will always be the read page. I wanted to make it as acessible as possible for people to jump right onto the site and begin scrolling. Or tap the chapter they're on and just get going. I got the code for that set up, I just need to add the dang chapters.

    For the extra's page, there will be more blogs and such when the chapters come out, with stuff relating to the comic and whatever I feel like, pretty much. I would also like to add a page for fanart if I get any; that's what the comment section is for, or any suggestions to the site, really.

    For the about page, that's just a short description of me and links to other pages (social media/Patreon). For Patreon, I plan to have two tiers of subscriptions and commissions available for purchase. The first tier will grant access to reading comic chapters early and your chosen name in the credits for each chapter release you remain a subscriber, with the second more expensive tier granting that perk, but also a Mount Mango-themed goody bag with stickers and stuff. Still a process, but that's something I'll figure out when the second batch of chapters comes out -- got to take it day-by-day. I also plan on putting the social links on the nav-bar, too, for easy access (on the right side).

    The most recent development that I made that's cool is making the site look good on phones (or any other screen), because I figure most people will be using the website on their phone to read a webcomic. The drag/drop character doesn't work on phones, but computer. I'll see if that's something I'll fix, or just leave it to make people want to go on the computer to read. The point of that is to let the reader mess around with icons or decorations to make the experience sillier. It could also be a way for people to share reactions to certain panels/events in the comic (when I add emoji icons).

    But overall, it's been a process. One step after another, figuring out how to add one function to the site after another with the help of YouTube tutorials, StackOverflow, and other coding websites. There was a point I thought I couldn't do any of this, well, fortunately, I proved myself wrong.

    The comic roll out. I am going to release the first seven chapters of The Mystery of Mount Mango to my website, Webtoon, and Tapas. It feels amazing to know I have my own avenue of control when it comes to my work, being this site. Before the launch, I am going to make cool videos to advertise it (as well as finish the last chapters and revise others), which is the stage I'm at now. So... again, more waiting. But I'm going for this more consistently now and that means it will get done sooner. I can't wait to show y'all this story brewing in my head. And after the release of these first seven chapters, trust, I've got way cooler chapters, plotlines, characters, and ideas to share with you -- for free.

    I thought about what would be be better: releasing it all for free, or pay-per-chapter. I decided the better option is to make it all free because I want as many as people possible to read this masterpiece, dang it! And if you support me financially, then I'll make it worth it on Patreon! But this is a story that I've been working on... too long, and will work on even longer because I like telling stories. I WANT THIS TO BE MY LIFE DANG IT!! MAKING STORIES AND MAKING MY OWN SCHEDULE AND ALL THAT, BUT THE STORY NEEDS TO BE GOOD, RIGHT?! AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHHH!!!! I'M TALKING A LOT BUT IT'S MY WEBSITE! SO DEAL WITH IT!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! HEHEHEHEHEHEHEHHEEH >:D XD XD UWU RAAAHHH!!!!!!
