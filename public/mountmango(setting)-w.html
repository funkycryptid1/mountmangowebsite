Mount Mango (setting)

Mount Mango can refer to the comic series, or the setting where the story "The Mystery of Mount Mango" takes place, or a real mountain in Ethiopia.

If you're looking to read about the comic itself, click here.



Thunder... storms... an awakening...

The centerpiece of the epic! A mountain that looms as large as it's own planet, elevated at an insurmountable height out of

the Earth's core, it's existence and arrival, a mystery to many. On it are towns, cities, ecosystems, environments a-plenty!

A world of it's own on the world. If it helps, imagine an icecream cone, and the mountain is the cone - the icecream is, well,

Earth.


Legends surround the mountain, one popular belief is that a treasure, so sweet, incomprable to any gain on the Mainlands (the seven

continents) could ever amount to, lies at the top. People take the trek from their home countries to the mountain ports of call,

which exist on the coasts of almost every major country and some smaller ones, to take a trip to the mountain's shores, at

the chance to experience something otherwordly.



Some go for the idea of treasure, others to experience the flora and fauna that unaturally collide in the Mainlands, at least with

our limited sense of human perception and belief. New life, unknown life, wonderous creatures exist throughout along with something

that could strike some semblence to what you would call... magic.
