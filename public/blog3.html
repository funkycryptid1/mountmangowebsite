    Blog 3: comic merch?!

    June 17th, 2026 - About 7:44 PM

    MERCH? BEFORE THE COMIC IS OUT??!!!

    Nah, it'll come out in a Patreon shop after the release of the first chapters. I was drafting some designs for the products on my site tonight. The main tasks I got for this rollout includes figuring out advertisting, finishing those chapters, and well this!

    The store will have relatively simple stuff to start out, including stickers, pins, and keychains. Take a look at some of my designs!



    Here's different sticker designs I had in mind. Some of the main four characters, some side characters, with some basic variety. The stickers will be sold in packs of 5 probably. Still have to figure out pricing based on shipping and other costs.



    Here's some keychain ideas. You'll see mainly Melody and Nio featured here since I consider them to be the most main of the main characters.





    As you can see, I went with Melody for the keychain design. This will probably be the design I pick, but in truth, now is a bit far from when I get these ready for the shop. Still a lot of time till then, but it's always fun designing!!

    This blog was shorter, just mainly wanted to share what was going on. I'm currently working on the 6th chapter of the story and coming up with ideas for how to... well bring this story to the masses! I wonder if the chapters are enough to draw people in, but regardless, I am super proud of what I'm cooking up and I think there will be people that'll enjoy it. I had some big ideas for advertising that will likely go on the backburner for now (I'm not saying unless I actually do em), so I figure the best thing I can do now is work within my means and find creative ways to have an epic launch/rollout.

    Thanks for reading --
